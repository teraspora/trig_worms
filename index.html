Vermichrome - very brief guide

Vermichrome is a pure Javascript Web App that;s meant to be both fun and educational.

It gives you the facility to draw pretty patterns by iterating parametrically-defined 2d curves over time, with each "stroke of the pen" being a shape such as a polygon, a star or a ring.

It will start each time with three curves selected randomly from a total of about twenty currently, and randomly-deteremined shapes. You are free to select and deselect curves as you please, by clicking the checkboxes. Hit Clear to start afresh with your new selection.

You can control quite a few parameters, such as shape, order (number of sides or spokes), size, speed, colour, rotation etc..

Other effects like pulsing and waviness are there for you to experiment with.

To adjust the parameters for a curve, first select it from the dropdown bottom right.

Tip: hit Pause, select your curves, adjust their parameters, then just select one of them before hitting Play. When you are satisfied with the drawing, hit Pause, deselect the curve, select your next curve and hit Play again. Proceeding like this you can layer patterns on top of one another.

Note: to get changing colours, set the hue to -1. Then you'll see the Polychrome Speed slider, which lets you control the rate of colour change.
